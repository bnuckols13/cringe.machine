Cringe Processing Machine

Welcome to a gentle journey of self-compassion and healing. This process combines memory processing with bilateral stimulation and evidence-based self-compassion practices.

What you'll do:

✍️
Write about an embarrassing memory
🫁
Process it through breathing and eye movements (or skip ahead)
💝
Practice self-compassion using phrases, visualization, or body-based techniques
💌
Write yourself a love letter

You have complete flexibility to move at your own pace and use the approaches that feel most natural to you. This is your safe space for healing.

Step 1: Memory Reflection

Think of an embarrassing or cringe-worthy memory. Write about it here. Remember, this is private and for your healing only.

💝 Gentle reminder: It's okay if this feels uncomfortable - that's normal and part of the process. You're being brave by facing this with compassion.

Step 2: Memory Processing

Now we'll process this memory. Hold it gently in your mind while following the breathing and eye movement instructions.

Breathe
Look Forward
Breath 1 of 25

Hold your memory gently in mind. Let it be there without fighting it.

Step 3: Self-Compassion Practice

Now we'll cultivate genuine self-compassion using three different approaches. Feel free to adjust your posture for comfort - this practice benefits from physical ease.

Instructions: First, let your awareness fill your whole body like inflating a balloon with gentle attention. Feel your natural vitality and life force. You deserve kindness - nothing needs to be achieved or perfected to merit this care.

Choose phrases that feel meaningful to you:

May I be safe and protected

Let each phrase be like gentle waves touching the shore of your being. Don't force feelings - simply plant these seeds of intention with faith in their natural growth.

Instructions: Maintain gentle whole-body awareness while imagining yourself held in healing light. Keep imagery simple and helpful rather than elaborate.




See yourself completely held and pervaded by golden-white light that brings comfort, healing, and ease to every part of your being.

If visual clarity doesn't come easily, that's perfectly fine. Even a simple sense of light or warmth is enough.

Instructions: Feel or imagine warmth, softness, and care radiating from your body's energy field. Let this caring energy suffuse your entire being.

Feel gentle warmth spreading from your heart center throughout your whole body. Let this energy of self-care permeate every cell, every fiber of your being. You are worthy of this tender attention.

Focus on the felt sense of care and tenderness in your body. This embodied kindness is the foundation of all compassion.

Practice for: 5:00

Step 4: Letter of Self-Love

Finally, write yourself a love letter. Acknowledge your humanity, your growth, and your worthiness of love and compassion.

💌 Writing prompts to inspire you:

  • What would you say to a dear friend going through the same experience?
  • What are you proud of about how you've handled challenges?
  • What makes you uniquely worthy of love and compassion?
  • How have you grown from difficult experiences?
